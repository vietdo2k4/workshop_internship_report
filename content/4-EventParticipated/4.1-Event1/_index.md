# Event Notes: FCAJ Community Day 23/5

### Session Goals

- Exploring Generative AI trends integrated with Cloud technologies.
- Learning strategies for personal knowledge management and indexing.
- Investigating CloudFront architecture for faster content delivery and security.
- Gathering product design insights under tight hackathon deadlines.

### Speaker List

- **Tinh Truong** - Context is Everything
- **Hai Anh** - Friendly AI Assistant with Amazon Quick
- **Thinh Nguyen** - From Edge To Origin: CloudFront as Your Foundation.
- **Team VIB** - 36hrs with LotusHacks
- **Dao Duc** - Non-Determinism of "Deterministic" LLMs Settings
- **Cat Vy** - Enterprise-Grade Multi-Agent System

### Featured Content

#### Build a Second Brain - Managing Personal Knowledge

- A systematic approach to capturing and organizing information that makes knowledge easier to retrieve and build upon over time.
- Offloads cognitive load from memory, freeing up mental energy for deeper thinking and creative work.
- Tools like Notion or Obsidian were introduced as platforms for building your own personalized "second brain".

#### Friendly AI Assistant with Amazon Q

- Integrating Amazon Q to let users interact with databases through natural language instead of writing complex SQL queries.
- Automates routine data lookups and analytics, reducing dependency on backend teams for simple queries.

#### From Edge to Origin - CloudFront as Your Foundation

- A detailed breakdown of how CloudFront routes requests from Edge Locations back to Origin Servers in modern web architectures.
- Practical configuration tips to speed up content delivery, minimize latency, and enforce edge-level security.

#### 36hrs with LotusHacks

- A full recap of the UTMorpho product development journey across a grueling 36-hour hackathon sprint.
- Real lessons in team role distribution, on-the-fly problem solving, and delivering a working demo under deadline pressure.

#### Non-Determinism of "Deterministic" LLMs Settings

- Unpacking the counterintuitive reality that LLMs can still produce inconsistent outputs even with fixed temperature settings.
- Guidance on tuning and controlling inference parameters to get more stable, reproducible model responses.

#### Enterprise-Grade Multi-Agent System

- An architectural walkthrough of coordinating multiple independent AI agents to solve a FinTech credit scoring problem.
- Analysis of why multi-agent setups outperform single-model approaches in terms of flexibility, scalability, and fault tolerance.

### Key Takeaways

- **Architecture & Technology**: Gained insights into CDN configurations (Amazon CloudFront) for high-availability systems; learned LLM foundations and the benefits of coordinating multiple AI agents.
- **Experience & Design Thinking**: Understood the necessity of structured knowledge bases (Second Brain) for career development; product design must always align with real user requirements; learned collaboration strategies under pressure.

### Action Plan & Future Direction

- **Knowledge management**: Applying Second Brain principles to catalog academic and developer reference materials.
- **Technical engineering**: Experimenting with CloudFront to improve loading speeds for web apps.
- **AI development**: Exploring multi-agent patterns to build small helper agents for routine tasks.
